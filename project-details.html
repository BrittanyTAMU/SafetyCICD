    One Detroit Center

    One Detroit Center, also known as Ally Detroit Center, is a prominent skyscraper located in downtown Detroit, Michigan.

    It features a neo-gothic architectural style that stands out with its distinctive spires and a copper roof, which has patinated over time. Rising to a height of 619 feet with 43 floors, it became one of the tallest office buildings in Michigan. The building’s construction involved advanced techniques and materials to accommodate Detroit's weather, with a facade composed of granite and glass. Structurally, it was engineered to withstand strong winds and temperature fluctuations typical of the region, making it a durable addition to Detroit’s skyline and a significant example of modern architectural engineering in the city.

    Working with Walbrigde on the One Detroit Center project in Detroit has been an incredible experience, thanks to SafetyFirst. The platform's real-time incident reporting and comprehensive safety analytics have been instrumental in maintaining a safe and efficient construction site. SafetyFirst's tools for risk management and compliance have ensured that we stay on track with our ambitious schedule while preserving the historic integrity of the building.
